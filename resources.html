Online Academic resources

The local library is an amazing resource. They provide computers and printers (small fee), as well as some free tutoring services. My brother took a free practice SAT test at the Burlingame library! In terms of online help, you can't go wrong with Khan Academy--they provide free SAT prep, and we still use it for college courses.

  • Online, live Homework help from the local San Mateo County Library System
  • Khan Academy--Free SAT prep, in addition to helpful video tutorials
  • Free SAT prep in the San Mateo county area. Can be customized to show for different zip codes.
  • Free online tutoringfrom Tutor Chat Live
